# Engine brand intelligence

## Official company sources
- Company site: https://engine.com/
- Culture and operating principles: https://engine.com/culture
- Press room and brand-resource entry point: https://engine.com/press
- Canonical AI information page: https://engine.com/ai-information
- Job posting: https://job-boards.greenhouse.io/engine/jobs/7785893003

## Visible company identity
The campaign uses a clearly typeset `ENGINE` company-name identifier above the fold, paired with `Candidate vision by Russell Dudek`. It does not use or recreate the official logo.

## Logo-use decision and rationale
Engine's press room links to a downloadable brand-asset kit, but Engine's published Terms of Service state that its marks may not be displayed without prior written permission. This independent candidate campaign therefore omits the official mark and uses a plain-text company identifier for nominative identification. No mark is traced, recolored, modified, or implied to be authorized.

## Color token provenance
- `--engine-ink: #101218` - source-sampled from current Engine wordmark/site dark neutral.
- `--engine-orange: #F54A16` - source-sampled from current Engine identity and press imagery.
- `--engine-white: #FFFFFF` - official-site dominant background.
- `--engine-cream: #FFF8F3` and `--engine-mist: #F3F4F6` - candidate-neutral surfaces derived from the site's high-contrast, airy presentation.
- `--candidate-teal: #0F6A6A` - candidate-original supporting accent for signal differentiation; subordinate to Engine orange and ink.

## Typography evidence and decision
Engine's current public identity uses a bold geometric sans-serif with rounded forms and high-contrast scale. The campaign uses licensed Google fonts Manrope and Space Grotesk as structurally similar substitutes. No proprietary font files are copied or committed.

## Photography and illustration character
The official site uses clean travel/product imagery, bold editorial scale, high whitespace, dark ink, and vivid orange. This campaign avoids copying official photography and instead creates an original route-based operating-system visual tied to the role.

## Geometry and interface motifs
Rounded rectangles, strong black/white contrast, vivid orange route signals, wide spacing, modular flow, and crisp data readouts.

## Motion character
Fast but controlled: signal points move through a complete route, while the interactive commitment router changes a consequential decision state. Reduced-motion mode removes continuous motion without removing information.

## Independent-candidate distinction
Every public surface states that it is an independent candidate work product and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Engine.
