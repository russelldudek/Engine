# Brand source notes

Engine's official Press Room provides a direct brand-assets download for logos and approved imagery. The local asset `engine-logo-official.svg` is a non-destructive, size-optimized derivative of the supplied official dark icon-and-wordmark lockup, cross-checked against Engine's current Press Room and official 2024 rebrand announcement.

The mark is used only for nominative employer identification. It is not recolored, redrawn, distorted, animated, or merged with a candidate-created mark. Every surface identifies the work as an independent candidate product that is not affiliated with or endorsed by Engine.
